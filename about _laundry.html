Laundry is the process of washing clothing and other textiles. Laundry processes are often done in a dedicated room called a laundry room or utility room, where there is access to a water supply and a drain. Laundry may also be done in a commercial setting such as a laundromat or in a dry cleaning shop. The process of laundry includes washing, drying, and ironing clothes and textiles. Different types of fabrics and stains may require different cleaning methods and detergents. Properly caring for clothing through regular laundry can help to maintain their appearance and prolong their lifespan.
